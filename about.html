me

My name is Jessica.

I am a 24 year old designer and photographer in New Jersey. I graduated with a Bachelors in Fine Arts degree with a concentration in Graphic Design, as well as a minor in Photography from Monmouth University. I love creating functional and inventful aesthetics with my own personal touches of textures, photography, and typography. I am excited to continue to build my knowledge and experience in graphic design and photography.
